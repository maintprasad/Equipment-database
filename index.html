📋 Langkah 1 — Buat Spreadsheet Baru
Buat spreadsheet baru bernama Prasad Equipment Database. Buat 2 sheet:
Sheet 1: PARTS
id | unit | area | equipment | nama | partNo | qty | satuan | spec | vendor | harga | stokMin | kondisi | catatan | kode | images | lastUpdate
Sheet 2: MACHINE_HISTORY
id | tanggal | unit | area | mesin | jenisLaporan | deskripsi | akarMasalah | tindakan | sparepart | pic | status | downtime | durasi | source | lastUpdate
